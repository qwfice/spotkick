How the Engine Works

Not magic. Just math, history, and pressure.

1

Career Base Rate

We start with the player's lifetime penalty conversion rate. Every kick they've ever taken in professional football feeds into this number.

2

Context Pressure

Knockout stage? Shootout? Group stage? The same player performs differently under different pressure. We apply a context-specific adjustment.

3

Recent Form Blend

We weigh their last 5 penalties at 30% influence. A hot streak or a cold spell changes the prediction in real-time.

4

Directional Intelligence

We analyze where they score most and how that direction performs. Left, right, center — every angle has a probability.
